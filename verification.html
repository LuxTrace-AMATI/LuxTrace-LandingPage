Product Verification

Have concerns? Verify your LuxTrace products with the following steps!

Step 1: Download QR reader

Download any QR code reader app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

Step 2: Scan the QR code

Scan the QR code on the back of the product packaging. Each QR code can only be verified once.

Step 3: Wait for the reveal

Congratulations! Now you can see the detail about trace of the product, this information can help you determine the authenticity of your product.
